connect the world

fangame, game design, programming

connect the world is a suika inspired fangame. developed in 2023.

what is it

The original suika game has one simple mechanic. drop fruits on each other and they combine to be bigger fruits. jank physics lets a fruit get accidentally launched out of the container and it's game over.

in our case, we drop creatures onto a planet, no beaker walls, and gravity pulls it somewhat towards the center. the catch? the planet keeps spinning like no tomorrow. the creatures, weighing nonsensical numbers, get bounced and rolled around the atmosphere.
