boba

conceptual, game design

boba is a game concept I designed and was developing in 2019 - 2020.

the goal of the project was to create a comfy real time bubble tea shop simulation game.

core mechanics

inspiration

the game centers around these magical words called inspirations , which are the building blocks of a recipe. these inspirations can come from talking with customers or from tweety and are the key components of the player's explore aspect.

brainstorm

players have access to 2 types of inspirations; main and sub. the player can combine these inspirations to create new recipes.

menu setup

the player then sets a menu of the recipes he has unlocked. drinks have built-in stats such as base interest, base cost and tags. these values determine the demand and appeal of your menu, allowing you to attract different customers.

restocking

drinks are limited everyday (day? more information in the next section!). players need to restock their drinks, ideally based on how well they sell. for convenience, players are given the option to auto-restock since cruncing numbers isn't for everybody.
