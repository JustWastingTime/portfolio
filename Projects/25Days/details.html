25 days to christmas

game design, programming

25 days to christmas is a resource management game where the player takes the role of a temporary Santa Claus. Due to issues beyond his control he has not prepared any gifts at all for christmas this year. With only 25 days left, create gifts, manage your elves and dont let christmas be destroyed! developed in 2022.

mechanics

Resources

there are 4 resources a player needs to balance. at the end of the day, resources lower while present count increases. when the day ends with a resource being at 0 or 100, the run is over.

morale

the elves' morale determines how much presents are crafted at the end of the day

when the elves' morale maxxes out, they throw themselves a wild party

when the elves' morale bottoms, they revolt

funds

money

when the coffers are full, you'll be investigated for corruption! what?

when the funds are empty, file for bankruptcy?/p>

mr green's happiness

christmas isn't complete without a big bad green villainy dude

when mr green is too happy, he'll take your job

when mr green is angry, he'll destroy christmas

infamy

infamy determines your public notoriety and appeal to the masses

a max infamy would get intervention from foreign countries

a low infamy and people forget about christmas
